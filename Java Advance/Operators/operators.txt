Types of operators : 

1. Logical operators : && , || , ! . 

2. Bitwise operators : &(Binary AND) , |(Binary OR) , ^(Binary XOR) , ~(Binary one compliment) , <<(left shift) , >>(right shift) .

3. Assignment operators : = , +=, *= , -= , /= .

5. Arithmetic operators = + , - , * , / , % .

6. Pre increment & post increment operators = ++a & a++ .

7. Pre decrement & post decrement operators = --a & a-- .

8. Relational operators : == , != , > , < , >= , =< .
